Websites Goal:

The goal of this website is to allow users play an interactive game of Noughts & Crosses. it does this by having the game implemented on the website, in the "Noughts & Crosses" webpage. a map is also in use on the "About" page to ensure the user knows where to play the game to take full advantage of the hue lights in this room which play a role in the game.

Lights Key

The Lights will turn red when its Player 1s turn and will turn tellow when its Player 2s turn. when the game is over the lights will flash with the colour corosponding to the winning player.
